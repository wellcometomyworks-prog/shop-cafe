WELCOME TO MY COFFEE SHOP!

A coffee shop is a welcoming place where people come to relax, socialize, or focus on their work while enjoying a variety of coffee and beverages. It offers freshly brewed coffee made from carefully selected beans, along with snacks or light meals that complement the drinks. The warm aroma of coffee, comfortable seating, and friendly atmosphere make a coffee shop an ideal spot for meeting friends, studying, or simply taking a break from a busy day.

TYPES OF COFFEE

Arabica Coffee

Prep time: 30minutes

Details

  • Mild, smooth taste with a pleasant aroma
  • Higher acidity
  • The most widely consumed coffee in the world

    Instructions

  1. Select high-quality Arabica beans for better aroma and smooth flavor
  2. Store the beans in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.
  3. Grind the beans just before brewing to preserve freshness.
  4. Use medium to light roasting to maintain Arabica's natural acidity.
  5. Brew with the correct water temperature (90-96Â°C) for best taste.

Robusta Coffee

Prep time: 20minutes

Details

  • Strong flavor with a bitter taste
  • Higher caffeine content
  • Commonly used for espresso and instant coffee

    Instructions

  1. Choose fresh Robusta beans for a strong and bold flavor.
  2. Store the beans in an airtight container to protect them from moisture.
  3. Use a fine grind, especially for espresso preparation.
  4. Apply medium to dark roasting to reduce bitterness and enhance body.
  5. Brew with proper pressure or strong methods to balance the intense taste.

Liberica Coffee

Prep time: 30minutes

Details

  • Large beans with a distinctive aroma
  • Less commonly consumed
  • Grown only in certain regions

    Instructions

  1. Select high-quality Liberica beans with a strong, distinctive aroma.
  2. Store the beans in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
  3. Use a medium grind to suit its large and irregular beans.
  4. Apply medium roasting to highlight its unique smoky and fruity notes.
  5. Brew using drip or French press methods to fully enjoy its bold flavor.

Excelsa Coffee

Prep time: 30minutes

Details

  • Complex flavor, both fruity and sweet-sour
  • Mainly used in coffee blends
  • Considered a sub-variety of Liberica

    Instructions

  1. Choose fresh Excelsa beans to ensure a complex and vibrant flavor.
  2. Store the beans in an airtight container away from heat and moisture.
  3. Use a medium grind to balance its unique taste profile.
  4. Apply light to medium roasting to preserve its fruity and tart notes.
  5. Brew mainly for blending or with drip methods to enhance depth and aroma.
